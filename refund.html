Refund Policy

Eligible Refunds

Duplicate payment, failed order charged, technical billing error, and approved disputes are eligible for refunds.

Refunds are not available simply because a user no longer wishes to use a subscription, unless required by applicable law.

Refund Process

Refunds are processed to the customer's Stoliy Wallet. Customers can then withdraw to their preferred method.

Disputes

If a dispute is filed, funds are frozen until the dispute is resolved by our customer service team.
